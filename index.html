About us


Welcome! Body and Soul Yoga is a website to give people more information about the benefits of yoga, the different types and also tutorials that are availble to everyone regarding of fitness lever or ability.

Yoga has been around for many years and is practiced across the globe by millions of people. It is a group of physical, mental and spiritual practices or which aim to still the body and mind.

We are a group of certified yoga teachers based in Ballina, Mayo and our aim is to educate people on the benefits, both physically and mentally, of taking Yoga and incorporating into their daily lives. A simple fifteen minute yoga session can change your mood and your outlook on your day.

Benefits of Yoga



  • Increased flexibility
  • Increased Mobility
  • Protection from injury
  • Increased muscle and tone
  • Improved energy and mood
  • Helps maintain a balance metabolism
  • Improved athletic performance
  • Weight loss (as part of a nutritional and balanced diet)
